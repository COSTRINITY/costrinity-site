Questions?

What happens after I purchase?

You'll receive a license key (SNIPR-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX) and a download link for the SNiPr package. The key activates your instance and unlocks your tier's features. Setup takes about 5 minutes.

Is my private key safe?

100% safe. SNiPr runs entirely on YOUR machine. Your private key stays in your local .env file and never leaves your system. We can't access it — nobody can. That's the point of self-custody.

What are the system requirements?

Node.js 18+, a Solana RPC endpoint (we recommend Helius or QuickNode), and a machine that can stay online. Works on Linux, Mac, Windows, VPS, or Raspberry Pi.

Can I upgrade my tier later?

Yes! Contact us via Telegram and pay the difference. Your license key will be upgraded within minutes.

What's the refund policy?

Due to the digital nature of the product, we don't offer refunds after license delivery. We're confident you'll love it — but reach out if you have issues and we'll help you get running.
